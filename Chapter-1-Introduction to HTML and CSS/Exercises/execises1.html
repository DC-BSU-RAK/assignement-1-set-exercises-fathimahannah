Cloud Cream Cheesecake

A rich, creamy, and smooth classic cheesecake with a buttery biscuit crust, perfect for any dessert lover.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 200g digestive biscuits or graham crackers, crushed
  • 100g unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (optional)

For the filling:

  • 900g cream cheese, softened
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or 1/2 teaspoon vanilla powder
  • 3 large eggs, room temperature
  • 200g sour cream (or plain full-fat yogurt)
  • 60ml heavy cream

Method

  1. Prepare the crust:
    • Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).
    • Crush biscuits finely using a food processor or rolling pin.
    • Mix crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until it resembles wet sand.
    • Press into a 9-inch springform pan.
    • Bake for 10 minutes, then let it cool.
  2. Prepare the filling:
    • Beat cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
    • Add sugar, then flour and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
    • Add eggs one at a time, mixing slowly.
    • Mix in sour cream and heavy cream. Batter should be silky.
  3. Bake:
    • Pour filling into the crust.
    • Use a water bath: place springform in a larger pan with hot water.
    • Bake at 160°C (325°F) for 60–70 minutes.
    • Turn off oven, crack door open, let it rest for 1 hour.
  4. Chill:
    • Remove from oven and water bath.
    • Cool completely at room temperature.
    • Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.

Optional Toppings:

  • Fresh fruit (berries, mangoes)
  • Fruit compote
  • Caramel or chocolate sauce
  • Whipped cream
